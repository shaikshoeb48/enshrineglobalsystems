About us

Enshrine is a global, full-service, cross-industry, end-to-end company that offers advisory and fulfillment services to clients all over the world.We provide innovative engineering solutions and management consulting in various sectors to develop an organizational structure and businesses. We work on projects related to Automation, Product development, Design & Analysis, Electrification, Smart Mobility, Automobile, Energy, Information Technology, Testing, Validation, and other Consulting Services. Our teams ensure that budget overruns, delays in schedulingor design errors are avoided, and we take pride in delivering consistent results that help our clients achieve their objectives. Headquartered in Farmington Hills, USA, the company has footprints in India and Germany. We are capable of handling projects of any size and nature in any corner of the country and abroad.
